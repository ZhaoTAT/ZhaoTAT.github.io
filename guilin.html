Intro of Guiilin

Guilin has enjoyed the reputation of "the best landscape in the world" since ancient times. It is an important tourism destination city in China and even the world. It has two laurels, the national key scenic city and the famous historical and cultural city determined by the State Council. It is known as the Pearl of international tourism.

Guilin has beautiful scenery. The landscape represented by Lijiang River scenery and karst landform has the reputation of "four wonders" of green mountains, beautiful water, strange caves and beautiful stones. It is a typical representative and classic brand of China's natural scenery.

"Thousands of peaks surround the wild and one water embraces the city". The scenery is in the city and the city is in the scenery, which is the unique charm of Guilin city.

Scenery of Guilin

With rich tourism resources and unique urban charm, successive governments of Guilin have made great efforts to develop tourism, which has achieved leapfrog development. At present, it receives millions of inbound tourists every year. Guilin is one of the first batch of "China's excellent tourist cities"

It is "China's best charming city" and "China's quietest city", and it is also a city most suitable for human habitation with a high happiness index.
