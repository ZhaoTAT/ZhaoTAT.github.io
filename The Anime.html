The Japanese Anime

Japanese animation is the collective name of Japanese animation and manga. Japanese animation occupies an important position in the world with the innovation and interest of the plot. It is the leader of the world animation culture.

Japan is a typical animation power. Its animation development model has distinctive national characteristics without losing innovation and attraction. Animation is an important industrial chain in Japan, with few rivals in the world. The development process of Japanese animation can be roughly divided into four stages:

  • germination
  • exploration
  • maturity
  • refinement

Development history

  1. 1917-1945 - embryonic period
  2. 1946-1973 - exploration period
  3. 1974-1989 - mature period
  4. 1990 till now - Refinement period

The American Anime

The first American cartoon appeared in 1906 and experienced five stages of development until 2002. The development history of American animation is mainly divided into:

  • the pioneering stage
  • the preliminary development stage
  • the first prosperity stage
  • the dormant stage
  • prosperity stage

The animation market in the United States is very mature and continues to improve itself with the rapid development of the film industry. In the history of world animation, American animation occupies an important position. It has always led the trend and development direction of world animation. It has a relatively mature commercial animation operation mode, many successful studios and world-class talent resources for animation design and production. So it is a well deserved film and animation kingdom.

The chinese Anime

Chinese animation originated in the 1920s. In 1926, China's first animated film, making a scene in the studio, opened a page in the history of Chinese animation. In the 1940s, Wan brothers created the first feature film of Chinese animation Princess Iron Fan, which was released to Southeast Asia and Japan. It was warmly welcomed by people and paved the way for Chinese animation to go international. Chinese animation has been incomparably brilliant, which also proves that Chinese animation has strength and artistic laws suitable for its development. Looking at the development of Chinese animation in recent decades, we can see that Chinese animation has always been committed to a road with its own characteristics.
