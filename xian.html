Introduction of Xian

Known as Chang'an in ancient times, Xi'an is not only an important birthplace of Chinese history and culture, but also one of the earliest regions with developed human civilization. Xi'an has a history of more than 3100 years of city construction and more than 1100 years of national capital history.

It is the earliest, most dynasties and longest ancient capital of China. It is also a world-famous historical and cultural city and an international tourist city. People say that entering Xi'an is like entering a museum of Chinese History: scenic spots and historic sites are scattered everywhere; Rare cultural relics are as bright as stars.

Gold plaque and Danqing are pleasing to the eye; The artistic conception of ancient locust green brick is profound. A city is simply a flowing history. Xi'an's self-confidence, openness, atmosphere and inclusive humanistic temperament have created a situation of gathering and development of various religions.

Among the eight major Buddhist sects, the ancestral courts of six major sects are in Xi'an; As a landmark ancient building in Xi'an, the wild goose pagoda is the place where the eminent monk Xuan Zang of the Tang Dynasty stored and translated Buddhist scriptures brought back from the western regions.

Famen temple cultural scenic spot, which treasures the finger bone relic of Buddha Sakyamuni, is known as the "Buddha capital of the world"; Xi'an Huajue temple is the only Chinese Islamic temple in the world. It is the Louguantai where Lao Tzu, the founder of Taoism, wrote books and lectured. It is known as "the first blessed place in the world".

Beautiful Scenery in Xian

Xi'an records the prosperous civilization and constantly creates new dreams. The grand "Tang imperial city revival plan" is being implemented smoothly, and the continuous construction of new scenic spots makes the history in the book go Come out, let the underground history stand up and let the extraordinary years of the past reappear.

There is the largest music fountain in Asia in the north square of Dayan Pagoda. The royal garden architecture and large-scale waterscape performance of Datang Furong garden, the central sculpture landscape of Datang never night city, pedestrian street and cultural venues are even more prosperous scenes of Datang.

Xi'an is a city of folk customs. It's really leisurely to see farmers' paintings in Huxian County, listen to Qin opera in the yivulgar society of the century old theater, and search for calligraphy and paintings and four treasures of study in the antique street of Shuyuan gate.

Xi'an is a city of delicious food. In your spare time, you can also take a walk in Minfang and ancient streets to enjoy the endless fun of eating in Xi'an.
