Intro of Beijing

Beijing is a famous national historical and cultural city with a history of thousands of years. In history, Beijing was the capital of Five Dynasties. In more than 800 years since the Jin Dynasty, many magnificent palace buildings have been built, making Beijing the most abundant city with the largest number of imperial palaces, gardens, temples and tombs in China.

Among them, the Forbidden City in Beijing, also known as the Forbidden City, was originally the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing Dynasties. 24 emperors lived here. The architecture is magnificent and magnificent, perfectly reflecting the traditional Chinese classical style and Oriental style. It is the largest existing palace in China and even in the world, and a valuable cultural heritage of the Chinese nation.

The temple of heaven is famous at home and abroad for its reasonable layout and exquisite structure. It is the place where the emperors of the Ming and Qing Dynasties "worship heaven" and "pray for Valley". It is the largest existing ancient sacrificial architectural complex in China and the precious heritage of world architectural art.

The summer palace is a famous tourist attraction in Beijing. The Old Summer Palace is the most famous Royal Garden in China. Zhongshan is green and water green. It enjoys a high reputation in the history of Chinese and foreign gardens and has high artistic value. It is known as the "garden of ten thousand gardens".

The Ming Tombs are the largest imperial tombs in Beijing. There are the tombs of 13 emperors of the Ming Dynasty, especially the Ming Ding mausoleum excavated in modern times. It is huge and spectacular.
