Introduction of Shenzhen

Shenzhen is not the "first" Special Economic Zone, but the "first batch" of special economic zones. In 1979, the central government agreed to pilot run export special zones in Shenzhen, Zhuhai and Shantou of Guangdong Province and Xiamen of Fujian Province.

Shenzhen, currently a sub provincial city, is a first tier super large city with a permanent resident population of 13.43 million and a GDP of 2692.709 billion yuan in 2019. It can be called "a rich country"! All the achievements of Shenzhen today are entirely due to the reform and opening up.

The name of Shenzhen first appeared in historical records in the eighth year of Yongle of the Ming Dynasty. "Zhen" is the consciousness of ditch in the local dialect, and Shenzhen is named because of its dense water and a deep ditch beside the village.

Shenzhen was originally just a fishing village. Due to the needs of reform and opening up, Bao'an county was included and renamed Shenzhen in January 1979.

Shenzhen is a pilot area, so many reform policies were first settled in Shenzhen, and Shenzhen has therefore become the "bridgehead" of reform and opening up. The development history of Shenzhen is the development history of reform and opening up. He has witnessed all the processes of reform and opening up, which is equivalent to the epitome of reform and opening up.

Shenzhen plays a leading role in the whole country. Therefore, Shenzhen has not only made drastic reforms, but also carefully implemented preferential policies, so it has created a remarkable "Shenzhen speed", known as "China's Silicon Valley".

The special food and scenery in Shenzhen

There are 5 Top special food in shenzhen. Top1 is Oyster, Oyster is the most famous specialty in Shenzhen (formerly Bao'an county), and Shajing oyster is most famous in the world. Because the oyster field in Shajing is located at the intersection of salt and fresh water, oysters absorb a lot of floating organisms in the sea water, so the oysters here grow fatter and taste more delicious than other places.

Top 2 is Nanshan litchi,Nanshan litchi has the characteristics of soft, smooth, tender, juicy and sweet taste. It has high nutritional value, sweet taste and crisp meat. It has always been known as "the treasure of China" and is a famous Lingnan fruit at home and abroad.

Top 3 is Gongming roast goose, Gongming roast goose is a traditional famous food in Guangdong, belonging to the Cantonese cuisine. Gongming roast goose is famous all over the world for its excellent color, aroma and taste. The freshly baked goose is golden and bright, with crisp and delicious skin, fat but not greasy meat and strong aroma.

Top 4 is Jiwei shrimp, Jiwei shrim is the main aquatic product for earning foreign exchange in the West Township of Shenzhen. Jiwei shrimp produced in Xixiang is small, like the middle finger, transparent and slightly pitted. The shrimp skin is very thin, the meat is very tender, delicious and nutritious. Its protein content is comparable to that of Shajing oyster.

Top 5 is Shiyan pear, Compared with northern pears, Shiyan Shali has several characteristics: first, it is large. "The fruit is as big as a bottle", and the largest one is generally one and a half kilograms; Second, the juice is good. Pear meat is snow-white, crisp and crisp, without residue and much water; Third, it is particularly sweet. It tastes delicious and sweet.
